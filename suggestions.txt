### Honest feedback on that idea:

Adding an arrow on the **top left** is **not the best choice**. It can look confusing (some people might think it’s a back button or decoration). 

For cards that link somewhere, the standard and clearest ways are:

### Better options (ranked):

| Option | Where to put it | How clear it is | Recommendation |
|--------|------------------|------------------|----------------|
| **"Lihat Materi →"** text at the **bottom** | Bottom of card | Very clear | **Best choice** |
| Small arrow icon **bottom right** | Bottom right corner | Clear | Good |
| Whole card has **hover effect** (scale + shadow) | — | Good visual hint | Should add this too |
| Arrow on **top left** | Top left | Can confuse users | **Avoid** |

### My recommendation for you:

Since this is for high school students, make it **super obvious**. Do this:

1. **Add text at the bottom of each card**:
   - Indonesian version: `Lihat Materi →`
   - English version: `View Materials →`

2. **Add a hover effect** (very important):
   - When user hovers the card, it should slightly lift up + get a shadow. This is the most common way people know “this is clickable”.

3. **Optional**: You can also add a small arrow icon next to the text at the bottom right.
